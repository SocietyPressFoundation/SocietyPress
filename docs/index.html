SocietyPress Documentation

Welcome to SocietyPress, the membership management system designed specifically for genealogical societies, historical societies, and heritage organizations.

What is SocietyPress?

SocietyPress is a WordPress plugin and theme that helps you manage your society's members, events, and communications. Unlike generic membership plugins, SocietyPress includes specialized features for genealogy organizations:

  • Surnames researched — Track what surnames each member is researching
  • Research areas — Record geographic areas of interest (counties, states, countries)
  • Event management — Create classes, workshops, and meetings with registration
  • Leadership tracking — Manage board positions, committees, and terms
  • Member directory — Help members connect with others researching similar lines

Who Is This For?

SocietyPress is designed for:

  • Genealogical societies
  • Historical societies
  • Heritage organizations
  • Family history groups
  • Lineage societies (DAR, SAR, etc.)

If your organization tracks members who research family history, SocietyPress has the features you need that generic membership plugins don't provide.
